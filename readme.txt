Trancelantic Playlist is a cool plugin that is able to display your currently played song on your website through a widget.


== Description ==

Trancelantic Playlist displays the songs you are playing on your media player to your website. You could also display a list of your recently played songs. It can also show a collection of the album covers from the songs you listened.

One widget for 

* your current track
* list of your last tracks
* cover gallery of your last tracks

**Supported Media Player**

You need to have a last.fm account. last.fm will grab/scrobble all needed data from spotify, apple music, etc. and display it for you wherever you need it on your website, thanks to its widget.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/trancelantic-playlist` directory, or install the plugin zip file through the WordPress Plugin Directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Drag and drop a widget on your page.
1. Insert your lastFM username in the widget admin box.
